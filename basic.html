Iron Condors — Basic Course

The foundation of the Iron Condor strategy, built from the ground up. You will learn how options work, how spreads are constructed, and how a complete Iron Condor is structured, priced, and evaluated.

📋 25 Questions ⏱ 45-minute exam ✅ 70% to pass (18/25) 🏆 Certificate on pass

What This Course Covers

📌Options basics — calls, puts, premiums, and strike prices
📌Moneyness — ITM, ATM, and OTM options explained
📌The Greeks — understanding theta (time decay)
📌Buying vs. selling — obligations and rights
📌Credit spreads — bull put and bear call structures
📌Iron Condor structure — all four legs explained
📌Max profit, max loss, and break-even calculations
📌When to use an Iron Condor — market conditions

By the End of This Course You Will Know How To

  • Identify the four legs of an Iron Condor and explain the role of each
  • Calculate maximum profit, maximum loss, and both break-even prices
  • Determine whether an option is in-the-money, at-the-money, or out-of-the-money
  • Explain why Iron Condor sellers benefit from time decay (theta)
  • Recognize the ideal market conditions for deploying an Iron Condor
  • Distinguish between a credit spread (seller) and a debit spread (buyer)
