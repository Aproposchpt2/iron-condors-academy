Verify a Certificate

Every certificate carries an ID that encodes the recipient, course, score, and date. Paste the ID below—or open a ?id= link—to confirm that its embedded details pass the site’s integrity check.

The check runs entirely in your browser; no personal data is uploaded. An intact checksum detects mistyped or edited certificate IDs.

Verification scope: This static checker validates the ID format and embedded checksum. A server-backed issuer registry or digital signature is required for cryptographic proof that a certificate was issued by the Academy.
